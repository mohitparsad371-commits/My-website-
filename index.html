Frequently Asked Questions

What is the last date for ITR filing?

Usually, July 31st is the due date for individuals. For audit cases, it's October 31st. Late filing attracts penalties up to ₹5,000.

Do I need a GST number for online selling?

Yes, GST registration is mandatory for selling goods online through e-commerce platforms like Amazon or Flipkart, regardless of turnover.
