Frequently Asked Questions

How much do you charge for ITR filing?

Charges depend on the source of income (Salary vs Business). Please contact us for a custom quote starting from ₹500.

Do I need GST registration?

If your turnover exceeds ₹40 Lakhs (goods) or ₹20 Lakhs (services), GST is mandatory. Voluntary registration is also possible.

What documents are needed for GST?

PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, Photo, Bank Details, and Address Proof (Electricity Bill/Rent Agreement) are required.
